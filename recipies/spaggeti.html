Spaghetti alla Carbonara

Description

A classic of Italian cuisine, Spaghetti alla Carbonara is a delicious pasta dish with a creamy sauce made from eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and crispy pancetta. This combination creates a comforting and flavorful dish that will satisfy your pasta cravings.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Crispy pancetta
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs and mix in grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Fry pancetta until crispy and drain on paper towels.
  4. Drain the cooked spaghetti and immediately toss it with the egg and cheese mixture.
  5. Add the crispy pancetta, season with black pepper, and serve hot.
