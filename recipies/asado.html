Asado Argentino

Description

Argentinian BBQ, or Asado Argentino, is a grill masterpiece. It involves cuts of beef, such as sirloin and ribs, slow-cooked on the grill with a touch of salt and pepper. The meat is cooked to perfection, tender and juicy on the inside, with an irresistible smoky flavor.

Ingredients

  • Cuts of beef (e.g., sirloin, ribs)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Prepare your grill and let it heat up.
  2. Season the beef cuts with salt and black pepper.
  3. Place the seasoned meat on the grill and cook to your desired level of doneness.
  4. Remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  5. Serve the succulent grilled meat with your favorite side dishes.
